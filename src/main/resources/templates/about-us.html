About our project:


Provides registered users with the ability to create and manage livestock. BarnDoor keeps track of the name, ear tag, gender, breed, pedigree, herd name, date of birth, weight and vaccine records of an individual animal. Through HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JQuery and Javascript we created an easy to use frontend experience that gives users the power to update to track their entire livestock data in one app.
